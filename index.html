About this Blog

Thank you for visiting my blog! This blog is reflection of my study at School of Professional Teacher Education, Oulu University of Applied Sciences. I am especially interested in "learning by making" and teacher's role in such environments. Learning by making incldes building tangible artefacts with digital technologies, for example making robots or games.

In this blog, I use a term "Making" (*Capital M) to describe learning by making, including digital fabrication, computer-aided design, fabricating with machines and coding. In this blog, I focus on teaching for in-service and pre-service teachers' professional development in the area of learning by Making.
