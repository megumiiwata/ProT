About me

Hi!
My name is Megumi Iwata.
I am studying at School of Professional Teacher Education, Oulu University of Applied Sciences, Finland.

I have Master’s degree in Educational Science and Bachelor’s degree in Business administration. I am interested in educational technologies and maker education (visit my website about digital fabrication projects!).

My goal of this professional teacher training is to develop theoretical understanding of pedagogy and practical skills to support students’ learning.
